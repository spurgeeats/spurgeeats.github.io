Spurge Eats was conceived on a late night after enjoying another plate of Spurge’s (very delicious) beans and rice. “Could there be more?” I asked. “How might this chef’s talent be seen by more beans, more brussel sprouts, and more garlicky dishes?” Hence, this website was born.


On the homepage sits a growing, adaptive list of recipes that Spurge might find inspiring. Anyone can contribute, including Spurge himself, by linking URLs, images, or PDFs to the matching Are.na board. Next lies the notebook, where some of Spurge’s best personal recipes & foods we have enjoyed together are detailed. Finally, this description page offers a reminder of how it all came to be.


Cheers!
