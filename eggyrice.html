The eggy rice!! This one changed my life. I’m not sure what this dish is supposed to be called, but hopefully calling it eggy rice is not offensive. The first time you made this, I was astounded. So simple, and yet so delicious. So easy! Inject it into my veins. Again, you know how to make this, but I will repeat the recipe for documentation’s sake. One time you made two sauces to go alongside that were bomb - please advise on the ingredients.


rice

chicken or veg stock

eggs

furikake

soy sauce

avocado, optional

cilantro, chopped, optional

Get the rice going in the rice cooker with chicken or veggie stock, or water if neither are available. Once I used a miso veggie stock from Trader Joe’s that was truly chef’s kiss. Mix up an egg with a fork in a small bowl, and when the rice is “almost there,” mix in the egg. Cover again, until the rice cooker switches off. Put in a bowl, top with furikake, soy sauce, a few slices of avo, and a bit of cilantro, and enjoy. Multiple times.
