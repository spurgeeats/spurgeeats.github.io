Dump all the roast stuff in a large dish - glass, preferably, so it’s easier to check on - and being experimenting with spices. I don’t quite remember what I used but I know you have it in your cabinet, so that’s a hint. Go crazy! Go stupid! Mix it all up and bake it in a 425 degree oven. Make sure the thighs are facing skin-up so you get that crisp. Be generous with the olive oil. It might take an hour, it might take two. Take solace in knowing that it will reheat very well. For the garlic bread, wait until the garlic in the roast is golden brown and the cloves are soft. Leave some butter out in advance so it’s a bit soft and easy to mix with the garlic. Mix that shit up with some salt, to taste, spread and bake at 425 for a few minutes. Don’t broil — it burnt the garlic the one time I tried it and tasted terrible. Enjoy!
