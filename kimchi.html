I tend to overhype things. We know, we know. This is an exception to that rule. Because it’s a very very good sandwich. You said it yourself. And that was meaningful to me! Prepare, knowing that if you have constant access to all of these ingredients in your kitchen, you may find yourself eating this more often than is “healthy”.


kimchi

cheddar, grated or sliced

sourdough bread

butter

mayo

love

appreciation for simplicity

baby carrots (optional)

Begin heating your pan on medium. While that’s happening, slather a bit of mayo on all sides of your bread (2 slices). Make sure that cheese is ready to go, and pull your kimchi out of the fridge. Have a fork at the ready. Drop a bit of butter into the pan and once it has melted, lay both slices down. Put a generous amount of cheese on both sides. Now comes the waiting game - things will be looking good, smelling good, SOUNDING good (record sizzling for work), and the cheese will need a few minutes to melt. Once it is about 3/4 of the way melted, use a fork to lay some kimchi on one side. Then, use a spatula to flip the non-kimchi slice onto the kimchi slice. Press, flip, and repeat until things are just beyond golden brown. Plate, and cut in half. Add baby carrots, and maybe a mandarin, because they really do make things complete.
